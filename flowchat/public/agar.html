Australian-Made Cleaning Products That Deliver Results

For over 55 years, Agar Cleaning Systems has been a trusted name in commercial cleaning supplies across Australia. As a proudly Australian-owned and operated company, Agar manufactures high-performance cleaning products designed to help businesses clean better, work faster, and save more. Whether you’re managing an aged care facility, running a hospitality venue, or leading a commercial cleaning business, Agar’s wide product range has you covered.

At Agar, we believe in delivering more than just chemicals — we deliver performance. Each product is packed with high-quality ingredients for superior concentration, meaning less product is needed for maximum effect. This not only saves you money but also boosts the productivity of your cleaning staff.

Buy Cleaning Products Wholesale or Online — With Confidence

Whether you’re purchasing cleaning products wholesale for a large operation or shopping for cleaning products online, Agar provides a seamless experience from order to delivery. Our national network includes warehouses in Melbourne, Sydney, Adelaide, and Brisbane, supported by an extensive distributor network that covers both metro and regional areas.
